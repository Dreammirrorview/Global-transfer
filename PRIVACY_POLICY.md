## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect information that identifies you as an individual, including:
- **Full Name:** First, middle, and last name
- **Contact Information:** Email address, phone numbers, mailing address
- **Identification:** BVN, NIN, passport number, driver's license number
- **Date of Birth:** Age verification and account eligibility
- **Gender:** For regulatory and demographic purposes
- **Occupation:** Employment and income information
- **Marital Status:** For product eligibility and risk assessment

### 1.2 Financial Information
We collect financial information necessary for banking services:
- **Account Details:** Account numbers, account types, balances
- **Transaction Data:** Transaction history, payment patterns
- **Credit Information:** Credit scores, loan repayment history
- **Income Information:** Salary, business income, other earnings
- **Bank Statements:** Account statements from other banks
- **Tax Information:** Tax Identification Number (TIN)

### 1.3 Device and Technical Information
We collect information about devices used to access our services:
- **Device Type:** Computer, mobile phone, tablet
- **Operating System:** iOS, Android, Windows, etc.
- **Browser Information:** Browser type and version
- **IP Address:** Network location identifier
- **Location Data:** Approximate geographic location (with consent)
- **Device Identifiers:** Unique device identifiers
- **Usage Patterns:** How you use our services

### 1.4 Biometric Information
With your consent, we may collect:
- **Fingerprint:** For authentication purposes
- **Facial Recognition:** For identity verification
- **Voice Recognition:** For voice banking services
- **Behavioral Biometrics:** Typing patterns, device handling

### 1.5 Communications
We record and store communications with you:
- **Phone Calls:** For quality assurance and security (with notification)
- **Emails:** Correspondence and support requests
- **Chat Messages:** Online support interactions
- **Letters:** Written communications
- **SMS Messages:** Transaction confirmations and alerts

### 1.6 Third-Party Information
We may receive information from third parties:
- **Credit Bureaus:** Credit reports and scores
- **Government Agencies:** Identity verification, sanctions screening
- **Other Banks:** Account verification, transaction processing
- **Employers:** Employment verification (with consent)
- **Reference Agencies:** Background checks (with consent)

- **Service Improvements:** Enhance our products based on your usage

- **Communication:** Send newsletters, updates, and educational content

We use information to:
- **Service Optimization:** Improve performance and user experience

### 4.2 Service Providers
We share information with third-party service providers who:
- Process transactions (payment processors, correspondent banks)

We share information with regulatory authorities when:

- Responding to lawful requests from CBN, NFIU, EFCC, etc.

### 4.4 Financial Institutions
We share information with other banks when:
- Processing inter-bank transfers
- Verifying account details
- Conducting credit checks
- Participating in shared ATM networks
- Clearing and settlement services

### 4.5 Credit Bureaus
We share information with credit bureaus to:
- Report your credit history and repayment behavior
- Obtain credit reports and scores

### 4.6 Law Enforcement
We may share information with law enforcement when:
- Required by court order or subpoena
- Preventing or investigating fraud
- Responding to emergency situations
- Protecting the rights, property, or safety of GBN or customers

### 4.7 Business Transfers
We may share information if:
- GBN is acquired by or merged with another company
- GBN sells or transfers assets or business units
- Bankruptcy or liquidation proceedings

In such cases, your information will be transferred subject to the same privacy protections.

### 4.8 With Your Consent
We will share information when you:
- Provide explicit consent for specific disclosures
- Authorize us to share information with third parties
- Request us to provide information to others

### 4.9 What We Don't Do
We will never:
- Sell your personal information to third parties for marketing
- Share your information without your consent (except as required by law)
- Disclose more information than necessary for the stated purpose
- Share information with companies that don't meet our privacy standards

## 5. DATA SECURITY

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Strict access controls and authentication systems
- **Firewalls:** Network firewalls and intrusion detection systems
- **Regular Audits:** Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Employee Training:** Comprehensive security training for all employees
- **Security Certifications:** ISO 27001, PCI DSS compliance

### 5.2 Data Protection
We protect your information through:
- **Secure Storage:** Secure data centers with physical and electronic security
- **Secure Transmission:** SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers
- **Secure Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations
- **Secure Disposal:** Secure deletion and disposal of old data
- **Backup Systems:** Regular backups with secure storage

### 5.3 Incident Response
We have procedures to:
- Detect security incidents quickly
- Investigate and contain breaches
- Notify affected individuals
- Report to regulatory authorities as required
- Remediate vulnerabilities

### 5.4 Your Role in Security
You can help protect your information by:
- Keeping your credentials confidential
- Using strong, unique passwords
- Enabling two-factor authentication
- Keeping your devices and software updated
- Reporting suspicious activity immediately
- Not sharing personal information unnecessarily

## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Retention Periods
We retain your information for different periods based on:
- **Legal Requirements:** As required by law (e.g., 7 years for AML records)
- **Regulatory Requirements:** As specified by regulators
- **Business Needs:** For as long as necessary to provide services
- **Your Preferences:** Based on your consent and preferences

### 6.2 Specific Retention Periods
- **Account Information:** 5 years after account closure
- **Transaction Records:** 7 years after transaction
- **Credit Information:** As permitted by credit bureau regulations
- **Communications:** 3 years for general communications, 7 years for legal matters
- **KYC Documents:** 5 years after account closure
- **Security Logs:** 1 year for operational logs, 7 years for security incidents

### 6.3 Data Disposal
When we dispose of your information, we:
- Delete electronic data securely
- Shred physical documents
- Anonymize data for analysis where appropriate
- Maintain records of disposal activities

## 7. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

### 7.1 Right to Access
You have the right to:
- Request access to your personal information
- Obtain a copy of your information
- Know how we use and share your information
- Receive information about data sources and recipients

### 7.2 Right to Correction
You have the right to:
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Update outdated or incomplete information
- Request deletion of incorrect data
- Provide additional information to complete records

### 7.3 Right to Deletion
You have the right to request deletion of your information when:
- No longer necessary for the purposes collected
- You withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
- We are no longer required to retain it by law
- Processing is unlawful

Note: We may not be able to delete all information due to legal or regulatory requirements.

### 7.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You may request restriction of processing when:
- Accuracy of information is contested
- Processing is unlawful but you oppose deletion
- We no longer need the information but you need it for legal claims
- You have objected to processing

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to:
- Receive your information in a structured, commonly used format
- Request transfer of your information to another service provider
- Where technically feasible

### 7.6 Right to Object
You have the right to object to:
- Processing based on legitimate interests
- Direct marketing communications
- Automated decision-making (including profiling)

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
You may withdraw consent at any time:
- By contacting our Privacy Officer
- Through your account settings
- By following unsubscribe instructions in communications
- Withdrawal will not affect past processing

### 7.8 How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise your rights:
1. Contact our Privacy Officer
2. Provide identification and account details
3. Specify the right you wish to exercise
4. Provide relevant details and supporting information
5. We will respond within 30 days (may extend for complex requests)

## 8. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

### 8.1 What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device that:
- Remember your preferences
- Keep you logged in
- Track website usage
- Personalize your experience

### 8.2 Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for basic functionality
- **Performance Cookies:** Collect usage data for analytics
- **Functionality Cookies:** Remember preferences and settings
- **Targeting Cookies:** Deliver relevant advertisements (with consent)

### 8.3 Managing Cookies
You can:
- Accept or reject cookies through browser settings
- Clear cookies from your device
- Disable specific types of cookies
- Check our cookie policy for detailed information

### 8.4 Other Tracking Technologies
We also use:
- **Web Beacons:** For email tracking and analytics
- **Pixel Tags:** For marketing and performance measurement
- **Fingerprinting:** For fraud detection and security
- **Analytics Tools:** Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics (with appropriate safeguards)

## 9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

### 9.1 Age Requirements
Our services are not intended for children under:
- **18 years** for most services
- **16 years** for certain savings accounts with parental consent

### 9.2 Parental Consent
For accounts opened for minors:
- We require parental or guardian consent
- Limited access to certain services
- Additional security measures
- Restricted transaction capabilities

### 9.3 Information Collection
We do not knowingly collect information from children without parental consent, except for services specifically designed for minors with appropriate consent.
