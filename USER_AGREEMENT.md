## 4. ACCOUNT OPENING AND ELIGIBILITY

### 4.1 Eligibility Requirements
To open an account, you must:
- Be at least 18 years old (or 16 with parental consent for certain accounts)
- Provide valid identification documents
- Have a valid Bank Verification Number (BVN)
- Provide proof of address
- Complete account opening forms accurately
- Meet minimum deposit requirements (if applicable)

### 4.2 Documentation Required
You must provide:
- **Primary ID:** International Passport, National ID, Driver's License, or Voter's Card
- **BVN:** Your Bank Verification Number
- **Proof of Address:** Utility bill or bank statement (not older than 3 months)
- **Passport Photograph:** Recent, clear photograph
- **Additional Documents:** For business accounts, corporate documents are required

### 4.3 Account Classification
Accounts will be classified based on:
- Customer type (Individual, Corporate, SME)
- Risk category (Low, Medium, High)
- Transaction volume and value
- Products and services used

### 4.4 Account Activation
Your account will be activated upon:
- Completion of KYC requirements
- Verification of provided documents
- Initial deposit (if required)
- System setup and approval

### 4.5 Joint Accounts
For joint accounts:
- All account holders must complete KYC
- All account holders are jointly and severally liable
- Operating instructions must be specified
- All account holders must agree to terms
