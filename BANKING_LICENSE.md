## 2. AUTHORIZED BANKING ACTIVITIES

### 2.1 Core Banking Services
The Bank is authorized to:
1. **Deposit Taking**
   - Accept deposits from the public
   - Offer savings, current, and fixed deposit accounts
   - Issue certificates of deposit
   - Provide call deposit facilities

2. **Lending Activities**
   - Grant loans and advances
   - Provide overdraft facilities
   - Issue letters of credit
   - Offer trade finance services
   - Provide project financing

3. **Payment Services**
   - Process domestic and international payments
   - Issue debit and credit cards
   - Operate electronic payment systems
   - Provide mobile banking services
   - Offer internet banking platforms

4. **Foreign Exchange Operations**
   - Buy and sell foreign currencies
   - Process international wire transfers
   - Issue foreign exchange drafts
   - Provide forex advisory services
   - Operate correspondent banking relationships

5. **Investment Services**
   - Manage investment portfolios
   - Offer treasury management
   - Provide brokerage services
   - Issue government securities
   - Underwrite securities

### 2.2 Specialized Services
- **Corporate Banking:** Full suite for businesses
- **Retail Banking:** Personal financial services
- **SME Banking:** Small and medium enterprise support
- **International Banking:** Cross-border services
- **Digital Banking:** Fintech-enabled services
- **Trade Services:** Import/export financing

### 2.3 Prohibited Activities
The Bank is prohibited from:
- Trading in goods or merchandise
- Engaging in manufacturing activities
- Owning non-banking subsidiaries without approval
- Providing insurance services without separate license
